When Excellent is possible, Good is not an option
Introduction
Lancer’s Hub is a virtual office platform designed to provide flexible, collaborative, and cost-effective virtual office solutions for freelancers, startups, and remote workers in Ethiopia.
Statement of the Problem
Freelancers lack dedicated workspaces, which hampers productivity and professionalism. Startups face challenges securing affordable office spaces, and remote workers struggle with isolation and maintaining work-life balance.
Scope
Lancer’s Hub offers extensive functionality, including virtual coworking spaces, project management tools, secure file sharing, and video conferencing. However, its capabilities are bounded by the technological limitations of the platforms and tools used, and it may not fully replicate all aspects of physical office interactions.
Current System
Gather.town is a web-based platform providing virtual spaces for remote teams to collaborate, socialize, and hold meetings. It combines video conferencing with customizable virtual environments, creating an immersive experience akin to a physical office.
Proposed System
Lancers Hub aims to provide a virtual office environment leveraging modern technologies for real-time interaction and user engagement.
Functional Requirements
The functional requirements include managing user authentication, workspace creation, project management, real-time communication, and integration with external tools.
Non-functional Requirements
Non-functional requirements encompass scalability, performance, security, usability, maintainability, reliability, interoperability, and flexibility. These goals ensure the system meets user needs while adhering to best practices and standards .
